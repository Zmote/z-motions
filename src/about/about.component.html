For visual work, I usually use the entire Adobe Collection, from Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign to After Effects, Premiere and Animate. For Coding, I usually rely on the Jetbrains product family, from Webstorm, PHPStorm to IntelliJ IDEA, CLion and the sort. For 3D Work I usually use Autodesk Maya LT, Blender or Google SketchUP.
